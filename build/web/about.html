ABOUT

Asansol Engineering College Library consisting of a central library and six Departmental libraries which collectively support the teaching learning programmes of the institute. The library uses Libsys software package. The database of books available in the library is updated day to day basis and can be accessed through OPAC.

RULES

1. Total six books will be issued to every students, three books from the book bank for the semester and three from the circulation section retainable for a maximum period of 30 days.
2. Reference copies, CDs , Journals , Magazines are not lent to anyone , however these can be issued in the reference section on the basis of temporary issue.
3. The borrowers' should check the books thoroughly for missing pages, pictures etc. before leaving the counter while borrowing books.
4. No book in damaged condition will be accepted from the borrower.
5. Borrower should not write anything or disfigure the book in any way.
6. Loss or damage of book must be reported to the librarian immediately and the library will collect the current price of the lost/ damaged book or asked to replace by a new copy.
7. For the loss of Library ID Card students have to deposit Rs. 50.00 for duplicate ID Card.
8. Fine: Late fine beyond the due date will be Re. 1.00 per day per book.
9. Library Timings: 9:30 AM-7:00 PM
Issue and Return of Books : 10:00 AM-4:00 PM
